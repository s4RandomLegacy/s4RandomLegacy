Known Issues

Roller:

  • Marital Status and Secondary Career(s) are linked. Thus, whenever you roll marital status, it will auto-roll secondary careers, and Secondary Career(s) cannot be re-rolled on its own. (The button just doesn't work yet.)
  • Several outcomes include dependencies that have yet to be auto-generated. (e.g. The "part-time job" career outcome doesn't just roll a part-time job for you.)
  • You have to go to the Rules page to see what the outcomes even mean. (Eventually, you should be able to click the outcome and it will give you a pop up with the details.
  • I'm hoping to implement a filter list so you don't have to manually re-roll every time the outcome requires a pack you don't own.
  • An export button so you can save your rolls and the rules that go with them.

Other Issues:

  • The site is not responsive to the size of your browser.
